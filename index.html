About Us

The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, had constituted an expert committee to suggest guidelines for evaluation and procedure for certification of various specified disabilities. It was decided that eight sub-committees in the following categories should be set up:

  1. Locomotor disability including cerebral palsy, leprosy cured, dwarfism, acid attack victims and muscular dystrophy
  2. Blindness and low-vision
  3. Deaf and hard of hearing and speech and language disability
  4. Intellectual disability and specific learning disabilities
  5. Mental illness
  6. Chronic neurological conditions
  7. Haemophilia, thalassemia and sickle cell disease
  8. Multiple disabilities

The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare is the final authority to recommend guidelines on evaluation and procedure for certification of specified disabilities. Our platform is based on these guidelines and calculations of all our tests is in accordance with the official guidelines.

Empathy, not sympathy

Know the difference between the two!

We often tend to show wrong behaviour or attitude towards the physically and mentally challenged individuals. They do not wish to seek sympathy. Rather it hurts their feelings. Instead we should be empathetic towards them. There's a huge difference between the two terms. Understand them but don't show pity. Very simple yet this is seldom followed.

Mental Health

One of the most crucial topics which is often ignored.

It should be noted that not every disability is visible. It is very important to take care of our mental health just like we take care of our physical health. Never hesitate to share your problems with close ones. Do not shy away from seeking medical help when needed. It is not to be ignored. You can take a test on mental health through this platform in the section "Mental Illness" mentioned above.
